Our Commitment to Privacy

Open Goban is committed to protecting your privacy. This notice explains what personal data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your information.

We operate on the principle of data minimisation — we only collect what we need to provide the service and keep the community safe.

What Data We Collect

Data Type Purpose Retention
Email address Account identification, notifications While account active + 2 years
Profile information Matching with other members While account active
Skills & offerings Community matching While account active
Exchange records Credit tracking, dispute resolution 7 years (for audit purposes)
Messages Dispute resolution if needed 1 year after exchange
Usage logs Security, platform improvement 90 days

How We Use Your Data

We use your data to:

  • Operate the platform — create your account, display your profile to other members, track exchanges
  • Match you with neighbours — show your offerings to people who need them and vice versa
  • Maintain the credit ledger — record exchanges and calculate balances
  • Ensure community safety — identify and address misuse or abuse
  • Resolve disputes — provide information to moderators when conflicts arise
  • Improve the service — understand usage patterns and fix problems

We do NOT:

  • Sell your data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising
  • Share your data outside the community without your consent (except as required by law)

Community Data Scoping

Your data stays within the community. Information you share is visible only to:

  • Other community members — can see your public profile and offerings
  • Community administrators — can see additional details for moderation purposes
  • Platform operators — technical access for maintenance and support

If you belong to multiple Open Goban communities, your data is kept separate. Each community operates independently.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of your personal data
  • Correct — update inaccurate information
  • Delete — request removal of your data (subject to our retention requirements)
  • Export — receive your data in a portable format
  • Object — opt out of certain uses of your data

To exercise these rights, contact your community administrator or the platform operator.

Contact: For privacy questions or data requests, use the feedback button on the main page or contact your community administrator directly.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Access controls limiting who can view your information
  • Regular security reviews

However, no system is perfectly secure. You should also take steps to protect your account, such as keeping your magic link emails private and not forwarding them to others.

Changes to This Notice

We may update this Privacy Notice from time to time. When we make significant changes, we will notify you and update the version number and date at the top of this page.

Continued use of the platform after notification constitutes acceptance of the updated notice.
